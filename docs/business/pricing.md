# Pricing Model — PAGANINI AIOS

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Platform License (Monthly)
Per-fund pricing based on AUM (Assets Under Management):

| AUM Range | Monthly/Fund | Includes |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| < R$50M | R$5.000 | All agents, 10K queries, 5 users |
| R$50M - R$200M | R$12.000 | All agents, 50K queries, 15 users |
| R$200M - R$1B | R$25.000 | All agents, unlimited queries, 50 users |
| > R$1B | Custom | Dedicated support, SLA, custom agents |

### 2. Usage-Based (Pay-per-Query)
For clients who prefer variable cost:
- Simple query (semantic search): R$0,10
- Complex query (multi-agent, RLM): R$0,50
- Due Diligence report: R$50,00
- Regulatory impact assessment: R$25,00
- Full structuring proposal: R$500,00

### 3. Setup Fee
One-time onboarding:
- Corpus ingestion + ontology setup: R$15.000
- Fund configuration + covenant setup: R$5.000/fund
- Custom agent training: R$10.000/agent
- Integration (Slack, API, data sources): R$10.000

### 4. Support Tiers
| Tier | Monthly | SLA | Includes |
|------|---------|-----|----------|
| Standard | Included | 48h response | Email, docs |
| Premium | R$5.000 | 4h response | Slack, phone, dedicated CSM |
| Enterprise | R$15.000 | 1h response | On-call, custom development |

## BYOK Cost Pass-Through
LLM costs are 100% pass-through to client (they use their own keys).
We do NOT mark up inference costs. This is a competitive differentiator:
"You control your AI costs. We never touch your keys."
